• Try to find all the mines!
  • Left click: Reveal covered cell.
  • Right click: Flag covered cell (if you think there's a mine there).
  • Middle click (left+right click): On a revealed cell, to reveal neighbours!
    If there are no mines in the neighbours, the cells will be revealed.
  • Once you've flagged all the mines and cleared all other cells, you win!
